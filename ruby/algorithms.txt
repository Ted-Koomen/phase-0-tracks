Getting gas for car algorithm:

-Enter car
-Start car
-Check gas meter
-If gas is below 25 miles left on tank
		-Go to gas station
	else
		-continue day as planned
-Turn off car
-Upen gas cap
-Get out of car
-Walk to gas pump
-Insert debit card into pump
-Insert nozzle into cap
-Pull level on nozzle 
-Fill gas tank until full
-Put nozzle back in gas pump
-Close gas cap
-Re-enter car
-Turn on car
-Drive home
